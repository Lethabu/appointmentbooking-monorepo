# Product Requirements Document: Appointment Booking SaaS

## 1. Introduction

This document outlines the product requirements for the Appointment Booking SaaS platform. The platform is a multi-tenant solution for salons and spas in South Africa to manage their bookings, clients, and business operations.

## 2. Goals

- Provide a seamless booking experience for clients.
- Empower salon owners with tools to manage their business efficiently.
- Enable business growth through AI-powered marketing and analytics.

## 3. User Personas

- **Salon Owner:** Manages the business, staff, services, and products.
- **Client:** Books appointments and manages their bookings.

## 4. Functional Requirements

### 4.1. Tenant Management

- Each salon is a tenant with its own branding, services, and clients.
- Tenant-specific subdomains and custom domains.

### 4.2. Booking Management

- Clients can book appointments through a public-facing booking page.
- Salon owners can manage bookings through an admin dashboard.
- Real-time availability and scheduling.

### 4.3. Client Management

- Salon owners can view and manage their client list.
- Clients can view their appointment history.

### 4.4. Service and Product Management

- Salon owners can add, edit, and remove services and products.
- Services and products are displayed on the booking page.

### 4.5. AI-Powered Features

- AI assistant for customer support and booking.
- AI-powered marketing and analytics.

## 5. Non-Functional Requirements

- **Performance:** The platform must be fast and responsive.
- **Scalability:** The platform must be able to handle a growing number of tenants and users.
- **Security:** The platform must be secure and protect user data.
- **Usability:** The platform must be easy to use for both salon owners and clients.

## 6. Future Enhancements

- Integration with more payment gateways.
- Advanced marketing automation features.
- Mobile applications for salon owners and clients.

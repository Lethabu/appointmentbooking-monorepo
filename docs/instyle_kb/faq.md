**Middle & Side Installation** and **Maphondo & Lines Installation** are both **R 500** for 1 hour. If using a new wig, add **R 100** for customization (tint, pluck, cut).

### Can I get a refund if I don't like the colour?

### What if I'm allergic to chemicals?
We use **sulfate & paraben-free products**. Always inform your stylist during intake. We can also use client-brought products if preferred.

### Do you charge for consultations?
No! Consultations are **free**. We recommend a 15-min consultation before colour or major services.

## Hair Care & Maintenance

### How do I maintain my wig between visits?
1. **Weekly wash:** Use sulfate-free shampoo.
2. **Deep condition:** 1× per week.
3. **Argan Oil:** 2–3 drops daily for shine & frizz control.
4. **Sleep protection:** Bonnet or silk pillowcase.
5. **Avoid heat:** Limit blow dryer use; air-dry when possible.

### What's the difference between a lace wig and a full wig?
- **Lace (HD, 4×4):** Melts into natural hairline; looks grown. More expensive.
- **Full wig:** Complete cap; easier maintenance; less realistic hairline.

### Can I wash my wig at home?
Yes! Use sulfate-free shampoo, lukewarm water, and gentle motions. Air-dry on a wig stand.

### Why is my colour fading?
Causes: chlorine, sun exposure, heat styling, harsh shampoo. **Solution:** Use our Sulfate-Free Repair Shampoo and Argan Oil serum.

### Can I perm or relax my hair before an installation?
No! Relaxer can weaken your natural hair. Wait minimum **4 weeks** after relaxer before installing.

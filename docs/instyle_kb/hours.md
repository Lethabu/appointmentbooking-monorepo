## Booking Rules & Cutoffs

- **Last booking slot before close:** 2 hours minimum (allows cleanup + client care post-service)
- **Weekend surge:** Saturday/Sunday slots fill 5–7 days in advance; book early
- **Express services:** Wash & Blow Wave (45 m) can be booked same-day
- **Installation services:** Book minimum 7 days ahead (prep time required)
- **Group bookings:** 3+ clients qualify for 10 % discount; contact via WhatsApp

---

## Special Hours

### **Bridal & Event Packages**
- **Pre-wedding glam sessions:** Available by appointment up to 2 weeks before event
- **Extended hours:** Can arrange 7 AM–7 PM for wedding party (48 h notice required)
- **Trial sessions:** Complimentary with booking of full bridal package

### **Corporate Bookings**
- **Team events:** Discounted group rates; 2-week advance notice
- **Lunchtime express:** 12:00–13:30 slots available for 30-min services

## Appointment Confirmation

- **SMS:** Received immediately upon booking

## Walk-In Policy

- **Express services only** (Wash & Blow Wave, Blow Wave only)
- **No guarantee** of availability during peak hours
- **First-come, first-served** basis
- **Wait time:** 30 min–2 hours depending on queue

## Delivery & Curbside Service

- **Product pickup:** Same-day available (products added to appointment)
- **Hair delivery:** Order by 12 PM for same-week delivery within Pretoria
- **Payment on delivery:** PayFast, Yoco, or cash accepted
